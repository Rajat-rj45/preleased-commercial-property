Data Protection

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how enquiry information is collected, used, stored, and shared when you interact with this website.

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Who We Are

This website is used for commercial property enquiry and lead communication for pre-leased commercial investment options. The website is marketed by ABSOLUTE ASSETS LLP, Authorised Channel Partner.

Information We Collect

When you submit a form, call, click WhatsApp, or request details, we may collect information such as your name, mobile number, email address, selected property type, investment budget, preferred tenure, ROI calculator selections, enquiry source, and communication consent.

We may also collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, referring page, page interactions, and basic analytics data to improve website performance and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, share project details, arrange site visits, provide rental yield information, explain documentation, coordinate with authorized representatives, and follow up on your commercial property requirements.

We may also use aggregated or non-personal data to improve website content, lead quality, user experience, campaign performance, and service delivery.

Communication Consent

By submitting your contact details, you authorize our team or associated representatives to contact you through phone, WhatsApp, SMS, email, or similar channels for property-related updates. This communication may continue even if your number is registered under DND/NDNC, where permitted by your submitted consent.

Sharing Of Information

We may share enquiry details with internal sales teams, authorized channel partners, developers, CRM providers, form processing tools, communication service providers, or advisors only when required to respond to your enquiry or deliver requested information.

We do not sell your personal information as a standalone database. However, lead information may be processed through third-party tools used for form submission, hosting, analytics, CRM, or communication.

Third-Party Services

The website may use third-party services such as form submission providers, WhatsApp links, phone links, analytics scripts, map embeds, CDN resources, fonts, or icon libraries. These services may process limited data according to their own privacy policies.

We recommend reviewing third-party policies before using external links or tools.

Cookies And Tracking

This website may use cookies, local storage, analytics tags, or similar technologies to understand traffic, improve performance, remember preferences, and measure campaign effectiveness. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Data Retention

We retain enquiry information only for as long as reasonably required for communication, customer support, legal compliance, business records, dispute resolution, and service improvement. Retention periods may vary depending on the nature of enquiry and applicable obligations.

Data Security

We take reasonable operational and technical measures to protect enquiry information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. No website, email, form tool, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be fully secure, so users should avoid sharing highly sensitive personal or financial documents through public forms.

Your Choices And Rights

You may request correction, update, or deletion of your enquiry information where applicable. You may also ask us to stop promotional follow-up communication. Some records may still be retained where required for legal, compliance, or legitimate business reasons.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adults interested in commercial property enquiries. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website after updates means you accept the revised policy.
